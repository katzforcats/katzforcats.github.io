Shakshuka

I love Shakshuka! It is the perfect savory easy meal for anytime of the day and requires just a few basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 16oz tomato puree
  • 4 fresh eggs
  • 1 red bell pepper ripped into 2 inch pieces
  • Cumin
  • Fresh bread for dipping
  • Sauteed greens for serving

Steps

  1. Heat skillet to medium/high
  2. Sautee bell pepper and cumin for a couple minutes in olive oil
  3. Add tomato puree
  4. Let mixture cook for 2-3 minutes until thickening begins
  5. Crack in eggs and lower heat and COVER
  6. Lift lid 3-4 minutes later et voila!!
